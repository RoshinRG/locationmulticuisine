Our Story

Born from a passion for authentic Middle Eastern flavours and the rich culinary heritage of Tamil Nadu, Location Multi Cuisine Restaurant has been serving the community since 2016. Our chefs bring decades of experience from across the Middle East and Kerala.

Every dish is crafted from scratch using the finest spices, freshest ingredients, and time-honoured recipes passed down through generations. We believe food is more than sustenance - it's culture, community, and celebration.
